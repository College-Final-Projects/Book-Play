🎯 Sort By Me Explanation:

This feature finds players who match both:

  • ✅ Same Sports: Players who like the same sports as you
  • ✅ Overlapping Schedule: Players available at the same times as you

💡 Make sure to set your favorite sports in your profile and your availability schedule below!

Your Availability

📅 How to set your availability:

  • Check a day: Available all day on that day
  • Add time slots: Available only during specific hours
  • Multiple slots: You can add up to 3 time slots per day
Available to Play

🎮 Available to Play:

ON: Other players can find you when they use "Sort By Me"

OFF: You're hidden from "Sort By Me" results

Suggested Players

🔍 Search vs Sort By Me:

🔍 Search Bar

Shows ALL players matching your search term (name, username, sports)

🎯 Sort By Me

Shows ONLY players who match your sports AND availability times
